# Organizing Events and Workshops

## Hackathons and Competitions:

Organize in person and virtual hackathons to encourage developers to learn about and build with Fetch-NFT and the Audius SDK

## Webinars and Online Workshops:

Host regular webinars and workshops to educate developers on Audius and the latest blockchain trends

## Participation in Conferences:

Attend relevant conferences, both physical and virtual, to network with developers and showcase Fetch-NFT and the Audius SDK

## Developer Meetups:

Organize local and virtual meetups to foster a sense of community and provide a platform for developers to share knowledge and experiences
